EPIC! The Talk Show

Featuring students and teachers from all over the school, Epic: The Talk Show, is meant to spread positivity and promote social connectivity within the walls of J.N Burnett Secondary. Each episode features a different class, team, and club highlighting their achievements/initiatives this year. As the pandemic continues to separate our school physically, we hope our talk show brings us together in spirit. Our goal is to connect all the corners of Burnett and foster a sense of Burnett culture. (VIDEOS CAN BE FOUND BELOW)
